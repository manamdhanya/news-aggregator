Apple MagSafe Battery Pack Will Now Charge Your iPhone Faster: Here is How

Source - NDTV

Tech Desk

April 21, 2022, 10:41 AM UTC

Description

Apple is offering its new firmware update for the MagSafe accessory that promises to give you faster charging for compatible iPhone models.

Content

Apple iPhone users who bought the MagSafe battery pack can now charge their iPhones at a faster speed. The company has released a new firmware update that boosts the charging speed of the MagSafe battery pack.

More Info

Apple has released a new firmware update for its MagSafe battery pack, offering a significant upgrade to iPhone users. With this update, users can now enjoy faster charging speeds, making it even more convenient to power their devices on the go. The MagSafe battery pack, designed to magnetically attach to the back of compatible iPhones, was already popular for its seamless integration with Apple ecosystem, and now, with enhanced charging capabilities, it becomes an even more attractive accessory for iPhone users. This faster charging speed not only enhances user convenience but also improves the overall usability of the MagSafe battery pack, especially for those who rely on quick top-ups throughout the day. The update is automatically installed when the MagSafe battery pack is connected to an iPhone, ensuring that users can effortlessly benefit from the enhanced performance without any manual intervention. This improvement highlights Apple commitment to providing continuous improvements to its hardware through software updates.
